About Eunoia Connect

We believe art is not about the product — it's about the process, the confidence, and the connection.

Eunoia Connect is a youth-founded nonprofit based in California. We partner with FCSN to run weekly art sessions for children with autism spectrum disorder, Down syndrome, communication differences, and emotional regulation challenges. We see neurodiversity not as a barrier, but as a different and beautiful way of experiencing the world — and art as the language that lets every child be heard.

Our Story

In 2022, the art program that had served neurodivergent children at FCSN for years was at risk of ending. The original founder was stepping away, and without new leadership, the sessions — and the children who depended on them — would be left without a creative home.

Three high school students decided they couldn't let that happen. They reached out, took over the program, and built Eunoia Connect from scratch — handling everything from volunteer coordination to supply purchasing to partnering officially with FCSN. What started as an act of loyalty to a community became a registered nonprofit, now serving 10+ students every single week.

Our Partner

We are proud to partner with FCSN (Family Caregiver Support Network), a community organization dedicated to supporting families of children with special needs. Our weekly sessions are held at the FCSN partner location, ensuring students learn in a familiar, safe, and supportive environment.
